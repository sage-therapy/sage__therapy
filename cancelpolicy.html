Cancellation Policy

At Sage Therapy, we value your time and commitment to your well-being. To ensure fair access to appointments and respect for our therapists’ schedules, we have established the following cancellation policy:

Appointment Cancellations

  • Please provide at least 24 hours’ notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment.
  • Cancellations made with less than 24 hours’ notice may be subject to a cancellation fee equal to the full session rate.
  • No-shows will also be charged the full session fee.

How to Cancel

To cancel or reschedule your appointment, please contact us by phone or email as soon as possible. Our contact information can be found on the Contact page.

Exceptions

We understand that emergencies and unforeseen circumstances can arise. Please reach out to us if you experience an emergency, and we will do our best to accommodate your situation.

Thank You

Thank you for your understanding and cooperation. Your commitment helps us provide the best possible care to all our clients.
